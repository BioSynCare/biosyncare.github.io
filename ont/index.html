BioSynCare Ontology (bsc)

Namespace IRI: https://biosyncare.github.io/ont#

This page documents the BioSynCare ontology namespace for terms such as bsc:Technique, bsc:Session, and bsc:Outcome. The canonical RDF/OWL document is available as Turtle.

  • OWL (Turtle): /rdf/core/bsc-owl.ttl
  • SKOS vocabulary: /rdf/core/bsc-skos.ttl

Usage

Prefix declaration example:

@prefix bsc: <https://biosyncare.github.io/ont#> .
@prefix id:  <https://biosyncare.github.io/id/> .
