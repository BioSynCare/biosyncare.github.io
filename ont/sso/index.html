SSO Namespace

Namespace IRI: https://biosyncare.github.io/ont/sso#

This page documents the SSO (Sensory Stimulation Ontology) namespace terms such as sso:Neuromodulation, sso:AudioTechniques, and sso:Session.

  • sso-ontology.ttl — Canonical TTL for SSO terms (e.g., sso:AudioTechniques, sso:Neuromodulation, sso:Session). Preferred for linking and reuse.
  • sso-ontology-extended.ttl — Extended model including additional terms and annotations used historically in the project.
  • sso-initial.owl — RDF/XML snapshot (initial version) for archival and compatibility.
  • sso-updated.owl — RDF/XML snapshot (updated) for archival and compatibility.

Usage

Prefix declaration example:

@prefix sso: <https://biosyncare.github.io/ont/sso#> .
